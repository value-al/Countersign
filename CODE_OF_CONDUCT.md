# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, public or private
harassment, publishing others' private information without permission, and other conduct which
could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to
the maintainers via this repository's **Security → Advisories → Report a vulnerability** channel,
or by opening a discussion. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
